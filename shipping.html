Shipping Instructions

  1. Submit the recovery request form.
  2. Power off the device (do not charge liquid-damaged devices).
  3. Package securely with padding.
  4. Include your name and contact details inside the parcel.
  5. Use tracked shipping where possible.

Shipping address details will be provided after form submission.
