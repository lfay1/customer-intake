How to Send Your Device (Ireland)

Follow these steps to protect your device and maximise recovery chances.

Liquid exposure: keep powered off. Do not charge or attempt to “test” it before shipping.

1) Submit the request first

Please submit the Request Recovery form so we know what’s coming.

2) What to include

  • The device (phone/laptop/drive). Only include the charger if requested.
  • A note inside the box with: your name, email, and a short summary of the problem.
  • Do not include SIM cards unless you specifically want SIM data checked.

3) Pack it safely

  • Wrap the device in bubble wrap (2–3 layers).
  • Use a sturdy box (not an envelope).
  • Fill empty space so it cannot move.
  • Seal well with strong tape.

4) Shipping method

  • Use tracked shipping (recommended).
  • Keep the tracking number.
  • We will confirm receipt by email.

Shipping Address

For safety, we send the address after you submit the request (to reduce spam / random parcels). After your request, we’ll reply with the address and packing reminders.

International shipping (future-flexible)

If you are outside Ireland, submit the request and we’ll confirm the best shipping option.
