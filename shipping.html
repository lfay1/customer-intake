How to Send Your Device

  1. Complete the recovery request form.
  2. Power off the device (do not charge).
  3. Wrap securely using bubble wrap.
  4. Include your name and email inside the box.
  5. Ship using tracked delivery.

Shipping address will be provided by email after request submission. Devices are handled securely and confidentially.
