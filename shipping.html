Secure Device Shipping Instructions

How to Send Your Device

Please follow these instructions carefully to ensure your device arrives safely and can be processed without delay.

1️⃣ Prepare the Device

  • Do not attempt repairs or drying (especially for water damage).
  • Do not power the device on.
  • Remove cases, SIM cards, memory cards, and accessories.
  • Send only the device unless instructed otherwise.

2️⃣ Package Securely

  • Wrap the device in bubble wrap (minimum 2 layers).
  • Place it in a sturdy box (not an envelope).
  • Ensure the device cannot move inside the box.
⚠️ Important: DataRecoverX is not responsible for damage caused by inadequate packaging.

3️⃣ Include Inside the Box

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • A short description of the problem
