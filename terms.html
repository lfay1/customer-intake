All devices undergo an initial diagnostic assessment. No recovery work will proceed without customer approval of the proposed recovery plan and cost.

If the requested data cannot be recovered, no recovery fee will be charged. Diagnostic, handling, or shipping costs may still apply if stated in advance.

Recovered data is retained for a limited time after service completion. Customers are responsible for confirming receipt and backup of recovered data.
