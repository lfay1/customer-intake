Terms & Conditions

This is a starter terms page to protect a new Ireland-first service. It’s not legal advice. When you grow, consider a solicitor review.

1) Diagnostics-first process

We assess the device to determine feasible recovery options. We will communicate findings and the proposed approach before recovery work begins.

2) No Recovery, No Fee (recovery work)

If we cannot recover the requested data, you will not be charged for recovery work. “Recovery” means obtaining the data requested in a usable form. Partial recovery may occur; we will explain what was recovered before any payment is required.

3) Fees that may still apply

Depending on your job, some non-recovery costs may apply, such as diagnostics/handling, parts required for temporary access, or return shipping. We will confirm any such costs with you before proceeding.

4) Customer responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information about damage history and symptoms.
  • Do not attempt further powering/charging after liquid exposure.
  • Back up recovered data promptly once delivered.

5) Data confidentiality

We treat your recovered data as confidential. We only access what’s needed to verify recovery. See our Privacy Policy.

6) Risk and limitations

Data recovery and board-level repair involve risk. Some devices can fail further during diagnostics or recovery attempts due to existing damage. We will use care and professional procedures, but outcomes are not guaranteed.

7) Device return and shipping

We recommend tracked shipping. We are not responsible for carrier loss/damage. Return shipping options are confirmed during the job.

8) Ownership

You confirm you are the lawful owner of the device and data, or are authorised to request recovery.
