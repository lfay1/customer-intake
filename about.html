About DataRecoverX

DataRecoverX is an Ireland-based specialist data recovery service focused on secure, privacy-first recovery of personal and business data.

We handle recovery from phones, laptops, external drives, USB devices and storage media affected by physical damage, liquid exposure, corruption, or non-booting faults.

Our process is diagnostics-first and no-recovery-no-fee — you only proceed once recovery options are confirmed.
