About DataRecoverX

DataRecoverX is an Ireland-based, diagnostics-first data recovery service specialising in phones, laptops, tablets and storage devices. Our priority is protecting your data and giving you clear options.

What we stand for

  • Privacy-first: only handle what’s needed to recover your data.
  • Diagnostics-first: assess the safest recovery method before work.
  • Transparency: you approve the plan and cost before recovery work begins.
  • Realistic outcomes: no false promises — just clear communication.

Future-flexible

We start in Ireland, but the business is designed to support secure mail-in recovery as we expand.
