FAQ

What does “No Recovery, No Fee” mean?

You only pay for recovery work if the requested data is successfully recovered. Policies for diagnostics/handling and return shipping are confirmed before work begins. See Terms.

Do you guarantee recovery?

No — data recovery depends on damage type, encryption, and device condition. We do diagnostics first and then explain the realistic chances before you approve any work.

My device got wet — what should I do?

Power it off immediately. Do not charge. Do not use rice or heat. Submit a request and follow the shipping steps. Liquid damage can worsen quickly when power is applied.

How long does it take?

Turnaround depends on the job. After diagnostics, we’ll give an estimated timeline. Urgent cases can be prioritised where possible.

Will my data be private?

Yes. We treat all customer data as confidential, process only what is needed for recovery, and follow GDPR principles. See Privacy Policy.

Do you repair devices too?

We focus on data recovery first. If a repair is needed to access data (temporary or permanent), we’ll explain options clearly before proceeding.

What if I can’t share my passcode?

Some recoveries require device access. If you prefer not to share it, we can discuss alternative options, but it may limit what’s possible depending on encryption and device state.

Can you recover data from a completely dead device?

Often yes, depending on the failure. We may use board-level diagnostics and micro-soldering techniques to temporarily restore access for extraction.
