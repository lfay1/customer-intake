Frequently Asked Questions

What devices do you support?
Phones, laptops, tablets, hard drives, SSDs, USB drives, and memory cards.

Is there a diagnostic fee?
Diagnostics are required before recovery and may include a handling fee.

What does “No Recovery, No Fee” mean?
You only pay the recovery fee if the requested data is successfully recovered.

Can you repair devices?
In some cases, temporary or permanent repair may be required to recover data. Repair costs are quoted separately and only proceed with approval.
