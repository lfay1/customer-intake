Privacy Policy

At DataRecoverX, we take privacy and data protection seriously. This policy explains how devices, information, and recovered data are handled throughout the recovery process.

Device & Data Handling

Devices submitted for recovery are accessed solely for diagnostic and recovery purposes. Recovered data is never reviewed beyond what is technically required to verify successful recovery.

Temporary Storage

Recovered data is stored securely and temporarily to allow verification and customer confirmation. Once recovery is completed and the device or data is returned, recovered data is deleted unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Confidentiality

All recovery work is handled confidentially. Devices and data are never shared with third parties except where required by law or where explicitly authorised by the customer.

Data Retention

No long-term copies of recovered data are retained by default. Diagnostic notes and recovery metadata may be retained internally for operational and record-keeping purposes.

Legal Basis

DataRecoverX operates in accordance with applicable Irish and EU data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Contact

If you have any questions regarding privacy or data handling, please contact us using the details provided on the website.
