Privacy Policy

What we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, phone).
  • Device information and issue description.
  • Optional information you provide to help recovery (e.g., urgency, whether a passcode is known).

Why we collect it

  • To communicate with you about diagnostics, recovery options, and logistics.

How we handle your data

  • We treat recovered data as confidential and only access what is needed for recovery and verification.
